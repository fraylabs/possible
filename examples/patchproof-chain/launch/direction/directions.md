# PatchProof Remix directions

All three previews use the exact copy in `launch/positioning/copy-deck.md` at a `1440 × 900` comparison viewport. They change presentation only; product claims, behavior, documentation, safety boundaries, and verification stay fixed.

## Continuous Form

A fanfold verification record feeds through the page as if a terminal run became a physical continuous-form receipt. Violet carbon ink, icy paper, tractor holes, and a restrained line-feed motion make evidence preservation tangible. It identifies instantly with logs, receipts, and inspectable records.

## Evidence Stamp

A civic inspection-station composition uses oversized red approval geometry, navy condensed type, diagonal reading order, and tactile stamp interactions. It dramatizes the reviewer’s decision without implying that every result passes.

## Patch Panel

An anodized-aluminum patch bay turns each evidence source into a labeled socket and each claim into an output channel. Industrial grotesk type, silver surfaces, cobalt/orange signal lines, modular composition, and cable-routing interaction expose the claim-to-evidence mechanism.

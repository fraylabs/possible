## Scoring notes

- PatchProof wins because the pain is directly evidenced, the MVP can remain local and deterministic, and its proof surface is visual. Its whitespace score is only 3/5 because multiple receipt/audit products exist.
- Repo Contract Doctor is easy to build but heavily crowded; it should not be pursued as a generic health score.
- ExampleLens has a concrete technical pain but a narrower audience and mature validators.
- RunnerGap attacks real friction but needs deeper emulation to avoid shallow advice.
